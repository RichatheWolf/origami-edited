Interesting Facts about Camels:

  1. Camel's ears are furry
  2. Camel can move easily across the sand because of its specially designed feet
  3. When they find water, they will drink as much as possible.

Interesting Facts about Pandas:

  1. Pandas mainly eat bamboo, however, they do eat other things
  2. Sometimes, they will do handstands on trees to mark their territory
  3. Baby Pandas are actually pink in color, and their fur slowly turns brown and then black and white

Interesting Facts about Pigeons:

  1. Pigeons are more intelligent than people think they are
  2. They have been used in the old-time military as messengers
  3. They can pass a test of self-recognition

Interesting Facts about Siamese Cats:

  1. Most siamese cats are cross-eyed
  2. These cats are also lactose intolerant
  3. Siameses used to belong to the royal family of Siam

Interesting Facts about Killer Whales/Orcas:

  1. Orcas aren't actually whales
  2. They are one of the most intelligent creatures, among octopuses, dolphins, and humans.
  3. Orcas keep one half of their brain awake while sleeping

Interesting Facts about Dogs:
